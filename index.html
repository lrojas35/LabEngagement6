Zodiac Signs

In your natal chart, you will see zodiac signs occupying luminary, planetary, and house placements. In total, there are 12 zodiac signs that are separated into 4 elements: fire, water, air, and earth.

  • Fire Signs: Aries, Leo, Saggitarius
  • Water Signs: Cancer, Pisces, Scorpio
  • Air Signs: Aquarius, Gemini, Libra
  • Earth Signs: Taurus, Capricorn, Virgo

Planet Placements

The closer planets are to Earth, the more they have an impact on personality, these are called Personal Planets. As we move further away, we have social planets and generational planets.

  • Personal Planets: Mercury, Venus, and Mars
  • Social Planets: Jupiter and Saturn
  • Generational Planets: Uranus, Neptune, and Pluto
